Angel Awards 2014

The Angel Awards 2014 was an amazing time as we rubbed elbows with Philadelphia’s top business leaders, politicians and a few celebrities. We also enjoyed music, food and silent auction fun. Oh ! And the gift bags you would have fought for!! The night was extra special as we honored a few dynamic individuals who truly care and believe in our youth!

Our 2014 Honorees Include:

Ed Snider of Youth Hocey Foundation: A cuttng edge organization that uses hockey to build character and academic skills for high-risk inner-city boys and girls from Philadelphia and Camden, NJ
Nick Cannon: Host of NBC's "America's Got Talent"
Jeri Lynne Johnson: Founder of Black Pearl Chamber Orchestra
Dr. Steve Perry: CNN Education Contributor and Host of "Save My Son"
