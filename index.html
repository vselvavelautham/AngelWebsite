What is our Community Angels Program?

Our new pilot program will give local high school students the opportunity to participate in self-esteem and community building workshops throughout the school year and encourage them to become well-rounded community leaders and participants! Here’s a step-by-step overview of how it works!

1. Sign Up

Start by signing up for a program!
Students can choose from a variety of different programs that revolve around 4 key values: Education/Career, Self Esteem, Community Service, and Culture.

2. Complete Programs

Students complete their customized program to earn wings! (Our point-based system)

3. Earn Wings

Students accumulate wings to become an official Community Angel!

4. Become an Angel

Students are assigned a mentor, invited to attend the Community Angel Celebrity BBQ, and has the chance to be recognized for their work at the Angel Awards Gala.
